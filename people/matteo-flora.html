Matteo G.P. Flora

Founding and Board Members

Founder and CTO at The Fool srl (thefool.it) developing software for e-Reputation, e-Moderation and IP Protection. Matteo has been serving as Computer Forensics Expert and Visiting Professors forn Universites, Expert Witness in International Cases and panelist for International Events including PH-Neutral, Italian Parliaments, IISFA and IBA. He is Board Member in the Italian CS Society and member of the technical Committee of the Italian Privacy Observatory. On 2012 Matteo was selected by the US Gov. to be part of the prestigious IVLP (International Visitors Leadership Program) meeting companies and government Agencies all across US.
