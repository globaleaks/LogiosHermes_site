Marco Pozzato

Founding Members

Amazed by C-64 in 1983, got excited with RPL on HP48, fallen in love with linux and OSS in 1994, developing on Java ecosystem since late 90s: lost his brain with blackberry development and finally found peace of mind when HTC Magic brought a penguin in his pocket.
Started his job career as software engineer, designing and developing a distributed access control system for physical security and video surveillance. Manages R&D team in PW, designing secure telephony solutions.
Founder of OpenLabs LUG in Milan, contributed an open ZRTP java implementation and an android client for Globaleaks. Using Macbook since one year, but slowly trying to get back to lovely Ubuntu, contributing to mactel repo.
