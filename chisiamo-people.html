Fellows

The Fellows of the Hermes Center for Transparency and Digital Human Rights contribute pro bono to the activities of the center. Some of them, the "research fellows", perform research (legal, coding, scientific, media, advocacy, outreach) specifically for the Center. Fellows are appointed by the Board. Fellowships last for a calendar year and are renewable.
