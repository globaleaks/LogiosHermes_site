GLDroid

GLDroid is a mobile software client for Android that act as a submission client for GlobaLeaks, enabling submission of photos, audio and video on-field.
It can be used during an emergency situation, to collect evidence of urban decay or to silently snap information about a specific action of malpractice.
It does incorporate security features such as anonymous submission with Orbot (Tor), and face anonymization with Obscuracam, thanks to the Guardian Project.

Project Status:

  • GLDroid Advanced prototype has been tested with Orbot (Tor) and Obscuracam
  • It will be comlpeted and released after the release of GlobaLeaks 0.2 (with the new REST API)

GLDroid on Github: https://github.com/globaleaks/gldroid
