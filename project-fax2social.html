Fax2social

Fax2social started with the idea of creating a free service to anonymously receive analog FAX and get them published to web 2.0 social networks such as Dropbox, Twitter and Facebook.

A similar project named Speak2tweet has been done by Google to receive phone calls, initially by Egyptian people, and publish the recorded phone calls on a website.
The Fax2social project aims at providing support to citizens in countries where internet kill switch is implemented to block communications of democratic movements.
The system provides phone numbers from different countries to cover broad FAX reception capabilities.

Inbound Fax will be published to the following Web 2.0 tools:
Twitter Fax2social - http://twitter.com/fax2social
Dropbox fax2social - http://dl.dropbox.com/u/38408436/fax2social/$file
Google Docs Viewer - http://docs.google.com/viewer
Facebook Fax2social - http://www.facebook.com/pages/Fax-to-social

The use-case as tool to bridge analog networks to social network work as follow:

  • A fax is received by a Fax2social number
  • The fax is uploaded to Dropbox.
  • The fax can be viewed on Google Docs Viewer.
  • Anyone can get updated on incoming fax and spread interesting faxes on the Twitter Social Space.
  • Dropbox hosted faxes are posted on Facebook to encourage translation, discussion and spreading.

Security notice
Even though sending a fax in certain critical conditions may look dangerous from a security-oriented point of view, we think that in a so critical condition, like an internet kill switch getting information out of the country, the use of a tool like Fax2social can be really important.

Fax2social software makes all best efforts to anonymize received materials before posting them to the Internet. Be aware however that fax IS NOT SECURE and your local telephone company may be able to intercept your fax and identify you!

Fax2social project has already received 8 fax2email numbers from the following countries by a generous telecommunication company supporting the project: France, Ireland, Finland, UK, Denmask, Sweden, Norway and Italy.

While we were building Fax2social we realized that there are many conditions where Fax Whistleblowing can be a very important tool for transparency initiatives, we worked hard for providing fax lines where confidentially receive documents about malpractices and other forms of abuses. For this reason we'll work to add the support to submit faxes directly to a GlobaLeaks site, enabling Fax Whistleblowing hotline support.

Project Status:

  • Software prototype has been implemented along with a draft paper on Fax2social project
  • Late 2012 / Early 2013 the lead developer will get back working to finish the software
