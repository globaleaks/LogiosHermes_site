Big Brother Awards

Every year Privacy International and a growing number of affiliate human rights groups present the Big Brother Awards to government agencies, private companies and individuals who have excelled in the violation of our privacy.
The Big Brother Awards is coordinated trough its main site listing all initiatives around the world.
In Italy the Big Brother Awards events, have been historically handled within the national e-privacy conference since 2002 by the Winston Smith Project. Since 2013 edition the Hermes Center for Transparency and Digital Human Rights will support the organization of the Big Brother Awards in Italy, on technical and advocacy activities.
